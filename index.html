Featured Project

Smart Pen

An innovative device that redefines digital interaction by allowing users to write in the air. The Smart Pen translates physical gestures into digital text and commands with remarkable precision.

Leveraging an integrated accelerometer and gyroscope, the pen tracks its motion in 3D space. This data is fed into a custom real-time algorithm that recognizes character strokes, enabling a seamless and intuitive way to interact with digital devices.
