Featured Project

Smart Pen with Gesture Detection

This innovative project brings writing into the third dimension. The Smart Pen allows users to write in the air, translating physical gestures into digital text or commands.

Using an accelerometer and gyroscope, the pen tracks its movement with high precision. This data is processed in real-time by a custom algorithm that recognizes character strokes and commands. It's a step towards a more intuitive and seamless interaction with our digital devices.
